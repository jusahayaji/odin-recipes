Bulgur and Pomegranate salad

Recommended for health conscious but hungry af moments

Moderately quick fresh and colorful salad.

ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cup medium-grind Bulgur
  • 1 Pomegranate
  • Feta cheese
  • Red onion
  • Parsley
  • 3 Tbsp Lemon and 1 tsp lemon zest
  • 2 Tbsp honey
  • 1 garlic clove minced
  • pinch of ground cinnamon
  • 1 1/4 cup virgin olive oil
  • fine sea salt
  • pepper
  • 1 cup parsley chopped(plus more for garnish)
  • 1/2 cup mint chopped(plus more for garnish)
  • 3 Tbsp pistachios chopped

instructions

  1. Soak burgul in water for about an hour then filter water (no need to cook in water).
  2. Over a bowl, to catch juice, open pomegranate.
  3. In a small bowl combine the lemon juice, honey, garlic, cinnamon and pomegranate juice.
  4. Constantly whisking, drizzle olive oil until emulsified. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. In a medium bowl, combine the bulgur, parsley, mint and pomegranate seeds.
  6. Add the dressing and toss to evenly coat. Season with salt and pepper.
  7. Transfer the salad to a platter and top with the pistachios. Garnish with parsley and mint.
