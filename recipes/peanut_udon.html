Peanut Udon

Recommended for broke ass students

Quick and easy recipe for a warm and satisfying bowl of noodles.

ingredient

sauce:

  • 5 Tbsp peanut butter
  • 1 Tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 Tbsp rice vinegar
  • 1-2 Tbsp chilli oil
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 1/4 cup of hot water, plus more, if needed

for the stir fry:

  • 1-2 tsp cooking oil
  • 1 clove garlic minced
  • 1/4 tsp fresh ginger minced
  • 12-14 oz udon
  • additional hot water, as needed
  • 1/4 cup cilantro chopped
  • 1/4 cup green onion (green part only) sliced

for garnish:

  • 2 Tbsp peanuts finely chopped
  • sesame seeds
  • green onion
  • cilantro

instructions

  1. Soak udon in a large bowl of hot water.
  2. In a small bowl, combine sauce ingredients and stir. (will melt in the skillet later)
  3. Heat cooking oil over medium heat; add garlic and ginger-stir for 30-45 seconds, add sauce-stir until melted, add udon and toss to coat well.
  4. Add hot water tablespoon at a time, to loosen the sauce and coat udon well.
  5. Stir in cilanto and green onion (keep more for garnish)
